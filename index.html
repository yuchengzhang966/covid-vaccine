COVID-19 Global Dashboard from Jan 2020 to April 2021

Understanding the pandemic through data visualization

About Stringency Index: This composite measure, developed by the Blavatnik School of Government at Oxford University, tracks government responses to COVID-19. It combines nine indicators including school closures, workplace restrictions, and travel bans, scaled from 0 to 100 (100 = strictest measures).

Data sources: Our World in Data, Oxford University, Natural Earth
